Keep it tamper-resistant — stop VPNs, hotspots and workarounds

Safe Internet filters every enrolled device on any network — including a phone hotspot, because the device keeps using our encrypted DNS wherever it goes. To keep a child from switching it off, lock the device with its built-in parental controls:

  • Windows: put the child on a standard (non-administrator) account and keep the admin password.
  • Android: use Google Family Link to lock device settings and block app installs.
  • iPhone / iPad: set a Screen Time passcode and disallow profile removal. For the strongest lock, enroll the iPad in MDM.
  • Enroll every device the child uses — an un-enrolled device isn't filtered.

Honest limit: a device you don't manage, on mobile data, can't be filtered by any DNS service — that comes down to the parental controls above and supervision.

Learning Mode restricts a device to approved educational sites only. Ehel keeps device browsing summaries for 30 days so parents can review activity; only you and designated school staff can see your child's data. Removing a device deletes it from the filtering servers.
