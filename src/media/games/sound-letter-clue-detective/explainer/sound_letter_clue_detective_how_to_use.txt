Welcome to Sound and Letter Clue Detective.

In this game, you use three kinds of clues to find the correct Arabic letter.

The sound clue tells you what the letter sounds like.

The letter clue tells you about the written shape, such as dots or curves.

The mouth clue tells you where the sound is made, such as the throat, tongue, or lips.

First, listen to the clue. You can press Say clue again if you need to hear it one more time.

Then choose the Arabic letter that matches the clue.

If you are not sure, tap Hint. The hint changes the game to the mouth clue and removes some wrong choices.

Correct answers build your score and streak. Tricky letters go into the Review Tray so you can practice them again.

Listen carefully, look at the letter shape, think about the mouth place, and solve the clue like a detective.
