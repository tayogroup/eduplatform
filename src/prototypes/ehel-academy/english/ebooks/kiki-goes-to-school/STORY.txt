KIKI GOES TO SCHOOL
Grade 1 - Level 1
Kiki series, book 1 (Term 1, Unit 1: Welcome to School)

1. Kiki Goes to School. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Today was Kiki's first day of school. She had a new red bag.
3. On the path she met Musa. "Good luck, Kiki!" he said.
4. The school was big. Kiki felt shy.
5. "Welcome!" said Miss Twiga, the teacher.
6. Kiki sat on a bench next to the little elephant.
7. They learned to say hello. "Hello! Hello!"
8. They counted one, two, three!
9. At playtime, Kiki shared her sweet mango.
10. She made a new friend, the little ostrich.
11. Ring, ring! The school bell rang. Home time!
12. "I love school!" Kiki told her family that night.

Unit vocabulary reinforced: school, bag, teacher, bench, hello, count,
share, friend, bell, home.
Themes: first-day courage, welcome, making friends
Setting: the tree school on the savanna; Kiki's baobab home
Characters: Kiki the young vervet monkey (niece of Musa's monkey friend),
Mama, Papa, Miss Twiga the giraffe teacher (Musa's giraffe with reading
glasses), the little elephant and little ostrich as classmates, and a
Musa cameo on the school path.

Series notes: opens the Kiki trilogy (school, family, games) set in the
same storyworld as the Musa books. Musa cameos in each Kiki book.
