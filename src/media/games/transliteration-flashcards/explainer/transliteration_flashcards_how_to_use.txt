Welcome to Transliteration Flashcards.

Your job is to match each Arabic letter with the way we write its sound in English letters.

First tap Listen. Hear the Arabic letter sound carefully.

If you are in typing mode, type the transliteration, such as ba, ta, meem, or qaaf, then tap Check.

If you are in multiple choice mode, tap the answer that matches the sound.

Use Hint if you need a small clue about the letter shape, dots, or sound.

Use Show when you want to learn the answer without guessing.

Correct answers build your score and streak. If a card is difficult, use Review missed to practice it again.

Listen carefully, say the sound out loud, and connect the Arabic letter to its transliteration.
