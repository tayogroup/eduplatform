# Illustration prompt set

Duku farm series (Term 2). The current page-NN.svg files are original
vector illustrations; for painted pages use the image-generation tool in
illustration-story mode with the shared spec below. Per-page prompts: use
the page alt texts in shared/course-ui.js plus STORY.txt.

## Shared visual specification

- Original hand-painted Grade 1 picture-book illustration; soft gouache
  and colored-pencil texture with subtle paper grain.
- Same savanna storyworld as the Musa and Kiki books.
- Duku is a little gray donkey with long ears, a dark mane and a pale
  tummy. Koko is a plump rust-brown hen with a red comb. Gigi is a cream
  nanny goat with small horns and a beard. Chicks are tiny and yellow;
  wild birds are small and blue.
- Farm dressing: wooden barn with a thatch roof, rail fences, haystacks,
  seed rows, and a friendly smiling scarecrow with a straw body and
  brown hat.
- No text, logos, frames, watermarks, humans, scary expressions, or
  duplicated limbs.
