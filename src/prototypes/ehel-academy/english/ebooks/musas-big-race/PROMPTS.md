# Musa's Big Race - illustration prompt set

Book 3 of the Musa series. The current page-NN.svg files are original vector
illustrations; use these prompts with the image-generation tool in
`illustration-story` mode (plus musas-muddy-stripes/character-reference.png)
to produce painted pages in the established book style.

## Shared visual specification

- Original hand-painted Grade 1 picture-book illustration.
- Soft gouache and colored-pencil texture with subtle paper grain.
- Warm East African savanna: golden grass, flat-topped acacias, distant blue hills.
- Landscape pages with simple, uncluttered visual storytelling.
- Musa is a cheerful young plains zebra with a rounded muzzle, three bold mane tufts, large brown eyes, and a heart-shaped black stripe patch on his left shoulder.
- Recurring friends: a golden young giraffe, small African elephant, black-and-cream ostrich, and gray-green vervet monkey.
- Race-day dressing: a finish banner of colorful triangle bunting strung between two wooden poles.
- Continuity with books one and two: same character designs and savanna setting.
- No text, logos, frames, watermarks, clothing, humans, scary expressions, extra animals, or duplicated limbs.

## Page prompts

1. Cover: Musa and all four friends gathered under the colorful race-day bunting banner.
2. The five friends lining up near the banner on a bright race-day morning.
3. The monkey raising both arms to start the race while Musa, the ostrich, and the elephant burst into a run.
4. Musa running joyfully in front, with the giraffe, ostrich, and elephant small behind him.
5. Musa running past one grand old flat-topped acacia tree.
6. Musa making one clean joyful leap over the familiar small fallen branch.
7. The little elephant sitting fallen in the grass with soft dust puffs around her and a gently sad face, while the ostrich races on far ahead.
8. Musa stopped mid-race, looking back over his shoulder toward his fallen friend, the finish banner visible close behind him.
9. Musa running back to the sad little elephant; a small warm heart of feeling in the air.
10. Musa and the smiling little elephant running the last stretch side by side toward the distant banner.
11. The ostrich crossing the finish under the banner amid falling confetti while the monkey, giraffe, Musa, and the elephant cheer.
12. All five friends together under a subtle rainbow, the little elephant beaming beside Musa; warm light completes the trilogy ending.
