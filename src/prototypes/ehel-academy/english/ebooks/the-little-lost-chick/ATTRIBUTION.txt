Original Grade 1 story created for Ehel Academy, 2026.
Duku farm series (Term 2), set in the same storyworld as the Musa and
Kiki books.
Story: Ehel Academy
Illustrations: Ehel Academy Learning Studio - original vector
illustrations from the shared series kit in
tools/create-musa-ebook-illustrations.js.

This is an original work. Duku the donkey, Koko the hen, Gigi the goat,
the chicks and the wild birds are new characters; Musa and Kiki continue
from the earlier books, also original Ehel Academy works. No third-party
story wording or artwork was reused.
