DUKU MAKES A SCARECROW
Grade 1 - Level 1
Duku farm series, book 1 (Term 2, Unit 4: Making Things)

1. Duku Makes a Scarecrow. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Duku the little donkey lived on a green farm.
3. Koko the hen planted little seeds.
4. But the birds came to eat them. Oh no!
5. "Let us make a scarecrow!" said Duku.
6. Gigi the goat found a long stick.
7. Koko brought straw. Duku brought an old hat.
8. They worked and worked. Tap, tap, tap!
9. The scarecrow was done. It looked funny!
10. The birds flew away... but they looked hungry.
11. So the friends made a little garden just for the birds.
12. Now everyone had food. What a good thing to make!

Unit vocabulary reinforced: make, stick, straw, hat, work, done, garden, food.
Themes: making things together, kindness to the hungry (series motif)
Characters: Duku the donkey, Koko the hen, Gigi the goat, the little wild birds.
