THE LITTLE LOST CHICK
Grade 1 - Level 1
Duku farm series, book 2 (Term 2, Unit 5: On the Farm)

1. The Little Lost Chick. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Good morning, farm! The sun was up.
3. Koko the hen had five little chicks.
4. One little chick liked to hop. Hop, hop, hop!
5. At lunch, Koko counted: one, two, three, four... Oh no!
6. "Where is little Pip?" Everyone looked and looked.
7. Duku looked in the big barn.
8. Gigi looked by the pond.
9. Musa looked in the tall, tall grass.
10. Then they heard a tiny sound. "Peep! Peep!"
11. Little Pip was asleep in the soft hay.
12. "Safe at home!" said Koko. The whole farm was happy.

Unit vocabulary reinforced: farm, hen, chick, barn, pond, grass, hay, look, count, home.
Themes: the farm community, searching together, home and safety
Characters: Koko and her chicks (Pip), Duku, Gigi, and a Musa cameo helping the search.
Counting motif continues from Kiki Goes to School.
