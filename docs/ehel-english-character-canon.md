## The rule

**One name = one person = one role, across every grade.**

The defect this prevents is real and was widespread before the rebuild: Amal was
the protagonist in nine units and a grandmother in a tenth; Adam was the older
brother in Units 6 and 9 and the younger brother in Unit 8; three different names
held the teacher role in a single grade; and a character called Samira changed
name to Isaac mid-paragraph in 126 passages across Grades 2ג€“8.

A child reads a whole year of these passages in sequence. The cast has to hold.

## Introducing a new name

Don't, unless a genuinely new role appears. If one does: take it from the neutral
dictionary's secular list, check it appears **nowhere** in the grade first, and add
it here with its role before shipping.
