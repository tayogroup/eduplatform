# Musa Helps a Friend - illustration prompt set

Sequel to Musa's Muddy Stripes. The current page-NN.svg files are original
vector illustrations; use these prompts with the image-generation tool in
`illustration-story` mode (plus musas-muddy-stripes/character-reference.png)
to produce painted pages in the established book style.

## Shared visual specification

- Original hand-painted Grade 1 picture-book illustration.
- Soft gouache and colored-pencil texture with subtle paper grain.
- Warm East African savanna: golden grass, flat-topped acacias, distant blue hills.
- Landscape pages with simple, uncluttered visual storytelling.
- Musa is a cheerful young plains zebra with a rounded muzzle, three bold mane tufts, large brown eyes, and a heart-shaped black stripe patch on his left shoulder.
- Recurring friends: a golden young giraffe, small African elephant, black-and-cream ostrich, and gray-green vervet monkey.
- Continuity with Musa's Muddy Stripes: same character designs, same puddle clearing.
- No text, logos, frames, watermarks, clothing, humans, scary expressions, extra animals, or duplicated limbs.

## Page prompts

1. Cover: Musa and all four friends gathered happily around the big puddle after rain.
2. The savanna on a soft gray morning after night rain, dotted with fresh puddles; no characters.
3. Musa joyfully splashing through a shallow puddle in golden morning light.
4. Musa standing alert with ears up, listening toward tall reeds in the distance.
5. The little elephant stuck belly-deep in a wide muddy puddle with a gently sad expression.
6. Musa at the puddle's edge speaking kindly to the sad little elephant; a small warm heart of feeling in the air.
7. Musa calling as the giraffe, ostrich, and vervet monkey hurry over the grass toward him.
8. The vervet monkey offering one end of a long green vine while the little elephant grips the other end with her trunk.
9. Musa, the giraffe, and the ostrich all pulling the vine together in a cheerful tug line while the monkey cheers.
10. The little elephant popping free of the mud in a comic, safe splash that flings mud drops everywhere.
11. All five friends speckled with mud, laughing together beside the puddle.
12. Everyone splashing clean in fresh rainwater under a subtle rainbow, the little elephant beaming; warm light completes the ending.
