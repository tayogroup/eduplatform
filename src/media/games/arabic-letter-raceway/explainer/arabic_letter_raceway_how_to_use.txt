Welcome to Arabic Letter Raceway.

Your car moves when you match each clue to the correct Arabic letter.

Listen to the clue first. The clue may talk about the sound, the mouth place, dots, heavy letters, light letters, vowel letters, or alphabet facts.

Then choose the Arabic letter that fits the clue. Correct answers move your race car forward.

If you need help, use Pit stop to remove wrong choices and see another clue.

Try to finish all thirty laps with a strong score. Listen carefully, choose safely, and drive to the finish line.
