MUSA'S MUDDY STRIPES
Grade 1 - Level 1

1. Musa's Muddy Stripes. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Musa the zebra loved to run.
3. He ran past the tall giraffe.
4. He ran past the little elephant.
5. He ran past the swift ostrich.
6. He leapt over a fallen branch.
7. Then - SPLASH! Musa slipped into a muddy puddle.
8. Mud covered his stripes. Musa felt sad.
9. The vervet monkey brushed him with soft leaves. But the mud stayed.
10. The elephant sprayed Musa with cool water. Splash, splash, splash!
11. The ostrich fanned him. The giraffe found a warm, sunny place.
12. Musa's stripes shone again. "Thank you, friends!" he said. Then everyone splashed and laughed.

Themes: friendship, helping, feelings, resilience
Setting: East African savanna
Target: Grade 1 beginning readers and read-aloud use
