DUKU'S FIVE SENSES
Grade 1 - Level 1
Duku farm series, book 3 (Term 2, Unit 6: My Five Senses)

1. Duku's Five Senses. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Duku woke up. What a fresh new day!
3. He saw the bright yellow sun.
4. He heard the little birds sing.
5. He smelled the sweet mango tree.
6. He touched the soft, soft hay.
7. He tasted a crunchy carrot. Yum!
8. Then Kiki came to visit the farm!
9. "Close your eyes," said Kiki. "What do you hear?" "A bell!"
10. "What do you smell?" "A flower!"
11. "What do you taste?" "Sweet mango!"
12. Eyes, ears, nose, hooves and mouth. Five senses - hooray!

Unit vocabulary reinforced: see, hear, smell, touch, taste, eyes, ears, nose, mouth.
Themes: exploring the world with the senses, playing a guessing game
Characters: Duku, with a Kiki cameo who brings the guessing game (and the school bell).
