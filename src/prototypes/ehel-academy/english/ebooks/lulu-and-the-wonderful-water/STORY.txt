LULU AND THE WONDERFUL WATER
Grade 1 - Level 1
Lulu journey series, book 2 (Term 3, Unit 8: Wonderful Water)

1. Lulu and the Wonderful Water. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Lulu followed the little river.
3. The river ran down the hills. Splish, splash!
4. Rain began to fall. Drip, drop, drip!
5. Lulu hid under a big leaf.
6. The rain stopped. A rainbow came out!
7. At last - the great lake! It was so big and blue.
8. A little fish jumped. Hello, fish!
9. The little elephant was there too, splashing!
10. A white boat sailed by. "Hello, Lulu!"
11. Lulu drank the cool, clean water.
12. "Water is wonderful!" sang Lulu.

Unit vocabulary reinforced: river, rain, lake, big, blue, fish, boat, water, drink.
Themes: water in all its forms, wonder, the journey middle
Cameos: the little elephant splashing at the lake. The rainbow motif
continues from the Musa books.
