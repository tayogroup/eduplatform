LULU IN THE CITY
Grade 1 - Level 1
Lulu journey series, book 3 (Term 3, Unit 9: City Places)

1. Lulu in the City. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Past the lake was the big city. Wow!
3. The streets were busy. The buildings were tall.
4. Lulu found the market. So many mangoes!
5. Kiki and Mama were there! "Lulu! Welcome!"
6. They showed her the city park.
7. They showed her the big clock tower.
8. Ding! Dong! The clock sang to the city.
9. At night, the city lights came on. So pretty!
10. Lulu made a nest by the park lamp.
11. The city hummed a soft good-night song.
12. "New places, new friends," said Lulu. "But friends make every place home."

Unit vocabulary reinforced: city, street, tall, market, park, clock, lights, night, home.
Themes: new places, belonging, friends as home - closes the journey arc
Cameos: Kiki and Mama at the city market.
