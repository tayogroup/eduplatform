KIKI'S FAMILY DAY
Grade 1 - Level 1
Kiki series, book 2 (Term 1, Unit 2: Family Time)

1. Kiki's Family Day. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Kiki's home was in the big baobab tree.
3. This is Mama. This is Papa. And this is her little sister, Nia.
4. Mama cooked dinner. Kiki helped stir the pot.
5. Papa picked mangoes. Kiki helped carry them.
6. Little Nia dropped her banana. She cried and cried.
7. "Do not cry," said Kiki. "You can have mine."
8. The family ate dinner together. Yum, yum!
9. Papa told a story about a brave little zebra.
10. Mama sang a soft, sweet song.
11. Kiki hugged her family. "Good night, good night!"
12. Kiki slept and dreamed of her happy home.

Unit vocabulary reinforced: home, Mama, Papa, sister, family, cook, help,
eat, story, song, good night.
Themes: family love, helping at home, sharing with little ones
Setting: the baobab home, day into night
Characters: Kiki, Mama (flower behind her ear), Papa (bigger, darker
coat), little sister Nia. Musa cameos inside Papa's bedtime story
(a thought bubble of a brave little zebra).

Series notes: the sharing moment on page 7 deliberately echoes the Musa
series kindness motif, with the floating heart.
