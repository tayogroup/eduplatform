Musa's Big Race

Original Grade 1 story created for Ehel Academy, 2026.
Book 3 of the Musa series, after Musa's Muddy Stripes and Musa Helps a Friend.
Story: Ehel Academy
Illustrations: Ehel Academy Learning Studio - original vector illustrations
in the shared Musa series style, generated by
tools/create-musa-ebook-illustrations.js from the same character kit as
books one and two.

This is an original work. Character designs (Musa the zebra, the giraffe,
the little elephant, the ostrich, and the vervet monkey) continue from the
earlier books, also original Ehel Academy works. No third-party story
wording or artwork was reused.
