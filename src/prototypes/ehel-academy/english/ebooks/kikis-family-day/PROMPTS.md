# Kiki's Family Day - illustration prompt set

Kiki series (Term 1). The current page-NN.svg files are original vector
illustrations; for painted pages use the image-generation tool in
illustration-story mode with the shared spec below. Per-page prompts:
use the page alt texts in shared/course-ui.js plus STORY.txt.

## Shared visual specification

- Original hand-painted Grade 1 picture-book illustration; soft gouache
  and colored-pencil texture with subtle paper grain.
- Same savanna storyworld as the Musa books (see
  musas-muddy-stripes/PROMPTS.md and character-reference.png).
- Kiki is a young vervet monkey: gray-green fur, cream face with cheek
  tufts, big brown eyes, and a little red school backpack.
- Family: Mama (pink flower behind her ear), Papa (bigger, darker
  gray-green), baby sister Nia (tiny Kiki without a backpack).
- Miss Twiga is the Musa-series giraffe wearing small round glasses.
- The tree school: chalkboard on wooden legs, benches and a hand bell
  under a big flat-topped acacia. Home is a stout baobab with a round
  door and window.
- No text, logos, frames, watermarks, humans, scary expressions, or
  duplicated limbs.
