Welcome to Articulation Maze.

In this game, you learn where Arabic sounds are made in the mouth.

First, listen to the Arabic letter. You can tap Listen again whenever you need to hear it.

Then choose the mouth family basket that matches the sound.

Some sounds are made with the lips. Some are made with the front tongue. Some are made with the back tongue. Some start in the throat.

You can tap a basket, or drag the letter tile into a basket.

If you are not sure, tap Hint. The hint tells you what your lips, tongue, or throat should feel.

Correct answers build your score and streak. If a sound is tricky, use Review missed to practice it again.

Listen carefully, say the sound out loud, and match it to the right mouth place.
