THE BIG FRIENDS PARTY
Grade 1 - Level 1
Capstone crossover (Unit 10: My First English World)

1. The Big Friends Party. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. One bright day, Lulu flew home from the big city.
3. "Let us have a party!" said Musa. "A party for all our friends!"
4. Kiki came from school with Miss Twiga and the games.
5. Duku came from the farm with mangoes and sweet carrots.
6. The little elephant filled the puddle with clean water.
7. They made a long, long table. Tap, tap, tap!
8. "Hello! Hello!" Everyone said hello to everyone.
9. They played ball. They flew the kite. They took turns.
10. They ate. They sang. Mama sang the soft song.
11. At night, the stars came out. The chicks slept in the hay.
12. "Look at our world," said Lulu. "School and farm, water and city - and friends everywhere!"

Year-in-review by design: each page deliberately echoes one unit -
hello and school (unit 1), family and the soft song (unit 2), games and
taking turns (unit 3), making the table (unit 4), the farm food and
chicks (units 4-5), clean water (unit 8), the city on the horizon
(unit 9). The final page names the whole English world.

Characters: the complete storyworld - Musa and his friends, Kiki and
her family with Miss Twiga, Duku with Koko, Gigi and the chicks, and
Lulu home from her journey.
