Welcome to Sound Sniper.

Listen first. Your job is to tap the Arabic letter that matches the sound you hear.

The game has three passes. Each pass has twenty questions. Every question gives you six letter choices.

After the sound plays, you have three seconds to answer. If you tap the right letter, you score points and move to the next question.

If you tap the wrong letter, or wait too long, you will hear a beep. The correct letter will light up, and then the next question will start.

Use Pause if you need a break. Listen carefully, remember the mouth shape, and choose the letter.
