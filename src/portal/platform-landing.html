Platform foundation

Run branded education workspaces from one operating layer.

EduPlatform gives academies, institutions, marketplaces, and teacher-led programs a shared Moodle foundation with domain routing, workspace isolation, role-aware dashboards, live-session operations, communications, reporting, SQA, and deployment controls.

Built for platform operators

Use one foundation to launch and govern multiple education brands while keeping each consumer's data, identity, users, and workflows scoped correctly.

  • Consumer apps with brand, domain, support, route, and workspace settings.
  • Role-aware access for admins, teachers, parents, students, principals, support, and SQA.
  • Operational dashboards for live classes, communications, reports, finance, and follow-up.

Foundation model

One platform, many branded education experiences.

EduPlatform separates the foundation from the consumer experience. The platform owner manages shared infrastructure, while each academy, institution, marketplace, or teacher workspace presents its own identity, domain, routes, roles, dashboards, and learning operations.

1

Consumer Apps

Create academy, institution, marketplace, and teacher-facing apps with their own public landing, login, dashboard, routes, and support identity.

2

Domain Routing

Map shared-root or custom domains to the right consumer without exposing platform internals or breaking the public URL experience.

3

Workspace Isolation

Scope students, teachers, guardians, live sessions, materials, reports, and communications to the correct workspace.

4

Shared Operations

Keep deployment, SQA, diagnostics, support tools, access checks, and platform governance in one foundation layer.

Operational depth

From public inquiry to live-class follow-up.

The platform is built around real education workflows, not only page routing. It supports the handoff from inquiry, intake, placement, enrollment, learning, live class, review, parent visibility, support, reporting, and continuous improvement.

  • Student and teacher intake with review, placement, schedule, consent, and onboarding data.
  • Workspace dashboards for people, materials, sessions, series, parent access, reports, and student profiles.
  • Teacher workspaces for daily classes, post-class review, capacity planning, performance, and improvement plans.
  • Parent-facing hubs for schedules, feedback, homework, recordings, summaries, receipts, and trust evidence.
  • Support and communications workflows with privacy boundaries, participant checks, read tracking, and audit trails.
  • Deployment tooling for integration, staging, production, Bunny CDN output, diagnostics, and rollback-ready operations.
