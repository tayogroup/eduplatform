MUSA HELPS A FRIEND
Grade 1 - Level 1
Sequel to Musa's Muddy Stripes

1. Musa Helps a Friend. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Rain fell all night. The savanna was full of puddles.
3. Musa the zebra ran out to play. Splish, splash!
4. Then he heard a sad sound. "Help! Help!"
5. The little elephant was stuck in the deep mud.
6. "Do not be sad," said Musa. "Friends can help!"
7. Musa called the giraffe, the ostrich, and the monkey.
8. The monkey found a long, strong vine. The elephant held it with her trunk.
9. Musa pulled. The giraffe pulled. The ostrich pulled. "One, two, three!"
10. POP! Out came the little elephant. Mud flew everywhere!
11. Now everyone was muddy. They laughed and laughed.
12. "Thank you, friends!" said the little elephant. "Helping a friend is the best game of all."

Themes: friendship, helping others back, teamwork, kindness
Setting: East African savanna, the morning after rain
Target: Grade 1 beginning readers and read-aloud use

Continuity notes: direct sequel to Musa's Muddy Stripes. In book one the
friends helped Musa when he was muddy; here Musa leads the friends to help
the little elephant, completing the reciprocity arc. Same five characters,
same savanna setting, and the story again ends with everyone playing in
the puddle together.
