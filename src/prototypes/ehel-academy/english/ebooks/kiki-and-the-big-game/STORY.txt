KIKI AND THE BIG GAME
Grade 1 - Level 1
Kiki series, book 3 (Term 1, Unit 3: Fun and Games)

1. Kiki and the Big Game. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. It was play day at school. Hooray!
3. Kiki and her friends played with the ball.
4. The little ostrich ran fast. Run, run, run!
5. Kiki went high on the swing. Whee!
6. They flew a big red kite. Up, up, up!
7. Then the wind took the kite. Oh no!
8. The kite was stuck in the tall, tall tree.
9. "I know!" said Kiki. "Let us ask a tall friend."
10. The giraffe reached up, up, up. She got the kite!
11. "Thank you!" they cheered. Musa came to play too.
12. Everyone took turns. Games are best with friends!

Unit vocabulary reinforced: play, ball, run, swing, kite, up, wind,
tall, turns, friends, fun.
Themes: play, taking turns, asking for help, friendship
Setting: the school playground under the acacias
Characters: Kiki, the little elephant and little ostrich, Miss Twiga,
the tall giraffe, and a Musa cameo who joins the games.

Series notes: closes the Kiki trilogy. The ask-a-tall-friend solution
mirrors how the friends helped each other in the Musa books - problems
are solved together in this storyworld.
