MUSA'S BIG RACE
Grade 1 - Level 1
Book 3 of the Musa series (after Musa's Muddy Stripes and Musa Helps a Friend)

1. Musa's Big Race. Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. It was race day on the savanna. All the friends came to run.
3. "Ready, steady, go!" called the monkey.
4. Musa ran fast. He was in front!
5. He ran past the big acacia tree.
6. He leapt over the fallen branch.
7. Then - BUMP! The little elephant tripped and fell.
8. Musa stopped. The finish line was so close!
9. Musa ran back. "Are you hurt, my friend?" he asked.
10. He helped her up. They ran the last part together.
11. The ostrich won the race. Everyone cheered and cheered!
12. "You stopped for me," said the little elephant. "You are a real winner, Musa."

Themes: kindness over winning, friendship, choices, sportsmanship
Setting: East African savanna, race day
Target: Grade 1 beginning readers and read-aloud use

Continuity notes: closes the trilogy arc. Book 1: Musa is helped by his
friends. Book 2: Musa leads the friends to help the little elephant.
Book 3: Musa gives up the win to help her again, alone and at a cost -
kindness has become who he is. Series motifs repeat on purpose: the
fallen-branch leap (book 1 page 6), the onomatopoeia page (SPLASH! /
POP! / BUMP!), the floating-heart comfort moment, and the rainbow
ending with all five friends.
