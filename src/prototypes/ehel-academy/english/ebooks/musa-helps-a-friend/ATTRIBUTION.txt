Musa Helps a Friend

Original Grade 1 story created for Ehel Academy, 2026.
Sequel to Musa's Muddy Stripes.
Story: Ehel Academy
Illustrations: Ehel Academy Learning Studio. This edition uses original
vector illustrations; painted versions matching the Musa's Muddy Stripes
style can be regenerated from PROMPTS.md with the studio image pipeline.

This is an original work. Character designs (Musa the zebra, the giraffe,
the little elephant, the ostrich, and the vervet monkey) continue from
Musa's Muddy Stripes, also an original Ehel Academy work. No third-party
story wording or artwork was reused.
