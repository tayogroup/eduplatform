Welcome to Letter Conveyor Belt.

Your job is to sort each Arabic letter into the correct basket.

Some letters are light. Light letters are read with a soft, easy sound.

Some letters are heavy. Heavy letters are read with a full mouth and a deeper sound.

Tap Start Letter Conveyor Belt. Then choose a moving letter on the belt.

After you choose a letter, you have five seconds to answer. You can tap the Light basket or the Heavy basket. You can also drag the letter into the basket.

If you are correct, the letter is sorted and your score goes up.

If you choose the wrong basket, or if time runs out, you lose a try and the letter keeps moving.

Listen carefully to the letter sound, look at the shape, and remember the heavy letters. Sort all the letters to win.
