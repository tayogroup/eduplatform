LULU SAYS LET'S GO!
Grade 1 - Level 1
Lulu journey series, book 1 (Term 3, Unit 7: Let's Go!)

1. Lulu Says Let's Go! Written by Ehel Academy. Illustrated by Ehel Academy Learning Studio.
2. Lulu was a little swallow. She lived by the big acacia.
3. It was time to fly to the great lake. "Let's go!"
4. "Goodbye, Musa!" called Lulu. "See you soon!"
5. She flew over Duku's farm. "Good luck, Lulu!"
6. Up, up, up went Lulu. The world looked small.
7. She flew fast. She flew far.
8. Then Lulu felt tired. Her wings were slow.
9. She stopped to rest in a tall tree.
10. A kind old bird shared her seeds.
11. "Thank you! Now I can go on," said Lulu.
12. "The great lake is near. Let's go, let's go!"

Unit vocabulary reinforced: go, fly, up, fast, far, slow, stop, rest, near.
Themes: setting off, journeys, kindness from strangers (series motif)
Cameos: Musa waves goodbye; Lulu flies over Duku's farm.
