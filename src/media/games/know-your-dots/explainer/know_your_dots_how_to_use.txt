Welcome to Know Your Dots.

Your job is to sort Arabic letters by their dots.

Some letters have no dots. Some have one dot. Some have two dots. Some have three dots.

First choose a letter tile. Look at the shape carefully. Listen if you want to hear the letter.

Then tap the basket that matches how many dots the letter needs. You can also drag the tile into the basket.

If you are correct, you score points and the letter is sorted.

If you choose the wrong basket, the game gives you a small clue and you can try again.

Look closely at the letter shape, count the dots, and sort every letter.
