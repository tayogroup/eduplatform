Kiki Goes to School

Original Grade 1 story created for Ehel Academy, 2026.
book 1 of the Kiki series (Term 1), set in the same storyworld as the
Musa books.
Story: Ehel Academy
Illustrations: Ehel Academy Learning Studio - original vector
illustrations from the shared series kit in
tools/create-musa-ebook-illustrations.js.

This is an original work. Kiki the young vervet monkey and her family
are new characters; Musa, the giraffe, the little elephant, the ostrich
and the vervet monkey continue from the Musa books, also original Ehel
Academy works. No third-party story wording or artwork was reused.
