# Ehel Academy — Religion-Neutral Character Name Dictionary

**Purpose.** Ehel Academy serves families from both Christian and Muslim backgrounds (and others). Character names must not mark a child as belonging to one faith. This dictionary is the **authoritative cast list** for all Ehel content — stories, examples, practice sentences, quizzes, activities.

**Selection rule.** Every name below is used naturally in **both** communities, so no child reads the cast as "not for me." Two sources of neutrality:

1. **Shared heritage names** — figures honoured in both traditions, so the name is common in Christian *and* Muslim families. Both spellings are listed; **use the neutral column** in content.
2. **Secular / cross-cultural names** — no religious association at all.

> **Do not use** names that mark one faith only: *Muhammad, Aisha, Fatima, Bilal, Hussein, Abdullah, Khadija* (Islamic-specific) or *Christopher, Christian, Mary-Grace, Angel, Trinity, Chastity* (Christian-specific).

## 4. Settings — religion-neutral only

| Use | Never use |
|---|---|
| home, kitchen, garden | mosque, masjid, church, chapel, cathedral, temple |
| school, classroom, library, playground | Sunday school, madrasa |
| market, shop, bakery | — |
| park, farm, beach, zoo, forest | pilgrimage sites, Mecca, Jerusalem |
| hospital, clinic, post office | — |
| bus stop, train station, town centre | — |
| community hall, sports centre | prayer hall |

**Celebrations:** use **birthdays, harvest, new year, sports day, school fair, family gatherings**. Avoid Eid, Christmas, Easter, Ramadan, Lent, Diwali.

**Food:** keep it neutral and inclusive — no pork and no alcohol (respects Muslim and many Christian families alike, costs nothing).

## 5. Illustration & media direction

- **Cartoon/illustrated figures only** — no photographs of real people.
- **Mixed race and skin tone** across the cast; vary hair texture and colour.
- **No religious dress or symbols**: no hijab, kufi, cross, crucifix, clerical collar, habit, prayer beads, prayer mat, headscarf-as-religious-marker.
- **No religious architecture** in backgrounds: no domes, minarets, steeples, spires, stained glass, altars.
- Everyday clothing: school uniform, t-shirts, jumpers, coats, trainers.
- Inclusive by default: show a range of skin tones, plus visible disability and glasses where natural.
- Alt text must describe the scene **without** faith or ethnicity labels — "A child pointing at a red book," not "A Muslim girl…".
